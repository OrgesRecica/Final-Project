Geneva
Geneva, Switzerland's global city, is renowned for its political significance and stunning natural beauty. Home to the United Nations and Red Cross, it’s a center for diplomacy. With the Alps as a backdrop, visitors can enjoy the serene Lake Geneva, explore the Old Town, or visit the iconic Jet d'Eau fountain.
Helsinki
Helsinki, the capital of Finland, is known for its modern design, stunning archipelagos, and vibrant cultural scene. With its unique mix of Scandinavian style and Russian influence, the city offers beautiful parks, innovative architecture, and a thriving arts scene. A blend of nature and urban living, Helsinki is both relaxed and sophisticated.
Berlin
Berlin, the capital of Germany, is a city of history, creativity, and transformation. From the remnants of the Berlin Wall to its booming arts and music scene, Berlin attracts visitors with its unique vibe. The city is a cultural melting pot, offering everything from historical landmarks to avant-garde galleries, making it one of Europe’s most exciting cities.
Frankfurt
Frankfurt, Germany’s financial hub, is known for its impressive skyline and vibrant arts scene. While famous for its banking sector, the city offers a rich history with landmarks like the Römer and St. Bartholomew’s Cathedral. A city that blends tradition and modernity, Frankfurt also boasts world-class museums and a lively culinary scene.
